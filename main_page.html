ABOUT

What we do

The LSE Global Society creates possibilities to engage with global issues at local venues. It provides a variety opportunities through its events for individuals to learn about topics that concern the entangled world and how they can get involved to solve them.

We host weekly meetings that are opened by inspiring speakers on global matters and are followed by lively discussions. For our events, click here.
Besides that, weekly focus groups work on their field of interest, prepare analysis and articles on current matters. Find out more here.
Our yearly conference brings together speakers and students from all over the world. To share their visions, experiences and stories. Topic and date for next year will be announced shortly. Are you excited about global matters? Do you want to get engaged? We are looking forward to meeting you - Contact us!

OUR PROJECTS

OUR PROJECTS

Our Project Meetings open with an expert giving the introduction and overview on the meeting's topic. The following exchange is supposed to question approaches, share own experiences and inspire that everyone gains new insights from them. Students from all backgrounds are welcome to join!


GLOBAL HEALTH

The Project Group Global Health is concerned with “health issues that transcend national boundaries and governments and call for actions on the global forces that determine the health of people.” (Ilona Kickbusch, 2006)
We are analysing current approaches by the international organisations such as the WHO and NGOs like MSF about the future health of the world population.


INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS

The Project Group discusses about current political developments and their impact on a globalized world.


GLOBAL HISTORY

In our Global History meetings historical case studies on the globalization of trade, ideas and people are focused. Experts present own research or leading work in their fields and set them in a global context.
